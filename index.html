About

Lesa McMaster started her training at the age of 6 with classical ballet. She continued her training as a dancer at Sydneyâ€™s McDonald College and then completed a Bachelor of Dance at the Victorian College of the Arts where she also discovered her love for yoga. After attending classes at Dance of Life, Breathe, Barre Body, Bikram and Ashtanga schools she enrolled at the Australian Yoga Academy in Prahran to complete her yoga teacher training. She also completed Meditation Teacher training at AYA / Melbourne Meditation Centre. Lesa loves to bring all the elements of her training together to create unique classes that are fun, engaging and respectful.

Classes include seated warm up postures, static & flowing strengthening standing poses, balancing postures, restorative poses, pranayama breathing and relaxation and are suitable for all levels of yoga practice, from beginners to advanced. With all Lesa's ballet training she has a focus in teaching correct alignment in poses & breath awareness.

Yoga classes are fantastic for strength and muscle tone, increased cardiovascular fitness, stamina, flexibility and energy, reduced tension and anxiety, lower levels of stress, and depression. Regular classes aid in increasing circulation & improving the functioning of internal organs and digestion. Musculoskeletal benefits include helping to prevent osteoporosis, reducing back pain and improving posture.

Book / Contact

Classes are limited to 10 people so it is helpful if you email info@refineyoga.com.au to confirm your place on the mat for a certain day & time. No bookings are required if you bring your own mat. Please arrive 5 minutes early to classes. Blankets are provided in the cooler months. If you have any health concerns please advise before the class. It is best not to eat for at least 2 hours before class. You might want to bring a hand towel, water bottle, jumper and socks.
